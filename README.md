Smart AI Study Planner is a streamlit based study schedule planner web app. The user can choose the priority level of the subject, provide the date of their exam, and specify the number of study hours available per day. Depending upon the above information, the app uses a priority-based algorithm to allocate time for the respective subject accordingly. In addition , the planner provides AI-powered study tips and suggestions to help students stay organized and prepare more effectively for exams.
